    Privacy & Data

    Your privacy is important to us. Here's how we handle your data.

    Complete Privacy

    All your data stays on your device. This website is designed with privacy as a core principle. We don't collect, store, or transmit any of your personal information or progress data.

    What We Store Locally

    • Progress Data: Which tasks you've completed, stored in your browser's localStorage
    • Certificates: Records of certificates you've earned, stored locally
    • Student Name: Optional name you provide for certificates, stored locally

    What We Don't Do

    • ❌ We don't send any data to external servers
    • ❌ We don't use cookies for tracking
    • ❌ We don't collect analytics or usage statistics
    • ❌ We don't share your data with anyone
    • ❌ We don't require any login or account creation

    How It Works

    This website uses your browser's localStorage feature to save your progress. This is a built-in browser feature that stores data locally on your computer. The data never leaves your device.

    If you use the website on a different device or browser, you'll start with a fresh progress state. This is by design - your data stays where you use it.

    Clearing Your Data

    You have complete control over your data. You can clear all stored progress and certificates at any time using the button below.

    This will permanently delete all your progress, certificates, and stored information. This action cannot be undone.

    Third-Party Services

    This website may link to external resources (PDFs, code examples) that are part of the course materials. These are read-only resources and no data is sent to them.

    When you generate certificates, they are created entirely in your browser. The print/save as PDF function uses your browser's built-in capabilities - no data is sent anywhere.

    Questions or Concerns?

    If you have any questions about privacy or data handling, please contact your course instructor.
